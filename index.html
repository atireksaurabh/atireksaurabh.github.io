Our Story

Two sunflowers bent towards each other

  • We Connected

    June 27, 2020

    He: A paragraph long introduction.
    She: Yes, I saw your profile!
    He(thinking): Faltu itna type kiya.

    But we continued chatting and soon we started giving dedicated time to each other.

  • Met Virtually

    July 25, 2020

    Video call, that was the only option. After some awkward silence, we were able to have some conversation and soon we lost track of time.

  • Virtual Date

    July 28, 2020

    Being in different cities, we planned to make use of the lockdown. And in the memory of SSR, we decided to watch the movie Dil Bechara on screen sharing.

  • First sight

    August 30, 2020

    He: Sorry, I am late by 7 mins.
    She:
    Bade Bhaiya: So you are the guy!

    And the rapid fire round begins.

  • The real meet

    August 31, 2020

    She finally speaks. And he finally learns the difference between a regular coffee and a medium coffee.
    Some silence, some talks and some magic. We realized we are much similar.

  • Parents' thumbs-up

    September 03, 2020

    Families took things further and agreed. But there was a long wait of 1.5 months due to some mythological restrictions for auspicious ceremonies.
    Mr. Touch-me-not and Ms. Chui Mui got some more time to know each other.

  • Rokafied

    October 19, 2020

    And finally the day arrived! He was at the hotel to receive her and family. Lift opens and she walked towards him in a maroon suit and he thought, "Am I seeing her for the first time".

    And finally the families met and we got rokafied!
